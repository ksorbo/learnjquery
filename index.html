About Us

Who we are:

What do all those numbers mean?

The purpose of this app is to allow you to view the most recent statistics from Project100Million.

  • Total Visitors - This is the number of visitors to all of our evangelism websites beginning in 2008.
  • Evangelism Response - The number of individuals who have come to our site, viewed a presentation and clicked one of our response buttons ("Yes, I prayed the prayer", "I still have questions," or "Prayer Request")
  • Discipleship Connection. - The number of visitors to our site who have not only clicked a response button but have entered their email address, name, gender and indicated which type of response they have made. Our volunteer connectors then make a personal contact with each individual to begin a Discipleship Connection.
  • Salvations - An individual has indicated that they have received Christ
  • Recommitment - An individual has recommitted their life to Christ
  • Question - An individual has asked a question
  • Prayer Need - An individual has submitted a prayer need.
  • Subscription - An individual has subscribed to one of our discipleship email series

Prayer Task Force

Prayer Task Force

Network211's Prayer Task Force is a group of people who have a burden to pray for needy people. Every week Network211 receives 100's of desparate needs from people all around the world. Our Connections manager reviews each needs and selects 8-15 needs each week. Members of the prayer task force receive an email with these needs once a week together with needs from the Network office.

If you would like to become a member of the prayer task force, please enter your name,email address and language preference below.
