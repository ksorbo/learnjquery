About Us

Network211

Our vision is to proclaim Christ to all people, build a global community of believers, and work with our partners in ministry.

Our mission is to use 21st century technology to communicate the 1st century Gospel. This mission is achieved by impacting our world and enabling our partners through these four strategic approaches:

  • Project 100Million is our strategy to engage 100 million people worldwide with the message of Jesus Christ. We interact with people, direct them to online discipleship, and help them find a local church.
  • Global Christian Center is our global online community where we seek to enrich the lives of believers, lead them in discipleship, and train them for service.
  • GCC Content Syndication empowers our ministry partners to use the Internet as a tool of ministry.
  • Global Friend Link encourages believers to reach out to others around the world through our Christ-centered social network.

It is with your partnership that we can present the message of Jesus Christ to the world. Presently for every $1 donated to Network211, we can reach 13 people with the Good News. Please partner with us today!

What do all those numbers mean?

The purpose of this app is to allow you to view the most recent statistics from Project100Million.

  • Total Visitors - This is the number of visitors to all of our evangelism websites beginning in 2008.
  • Evangelism Response - The number of individuals who have come to our site, viewed a presentation and clicked one of our response buttons ("Yes, I prayed the prayer," "I still have questions," or "Prayer Request")
  • Discipleship Connections - The number of visitors to our sites who made an evangelism response, and then completed a form and wrote to us. Our volunteer connectors then make a personal contact with each individual to begin a Discipleship Connection.
  • Salvations - Individuals who indicated that they have received Christ
  • Recommitments - Individuals who indicated that they have recommitted their lives to Christ
  • Questions - Individuals who submitted a question
  • Prayer Requests - Individuals who submitted a prayer request.
  • Subscriptions - Individuals who subscribed to Network211 discipleship series.
