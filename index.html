About Us

Who we are:

Project 100Million is an evangelism ministry of Network211. Our goal is to reach 100 million individuals with the Gospel using the Internet. Our intermediate goal is to present our Journeyanswers sites in the top ten languages of the Internet.

Help

What do all those numbers mean?

The purpose of this app is to allow you to view the most recent statistics from Project100Million.

  • Total Visitors - This is the number of visitors to all of our evangelism websites beginning in 2008.
  • Evangelism Response - The number of individuals who have come to our site, viewed a presentation and clicked one of our response buttons ("Yes, I prayed the prayer," "I still have questions," or "Prayer Request")
  • Discipleship Connections - The number of visitors to our sites who made an evangelism response, and then completed a form and wrote to us. Our volunteer connectors then make a personal contact with each individual to begin a Discipleship Connection.
  • Salvations - Individuals who indicated that they have received Christ
  • Recommitments - Individuals who indicated that they have recommitted their lives to Christ
  • Questions - Individuals who submitted a question
  • Prayer Requests - Individuals who submitted a prayer request.
  • Subscriptions - Individuals who subscribed to Network211 discipleship series.
